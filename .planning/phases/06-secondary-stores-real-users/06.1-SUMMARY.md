# Phase 06.1 Summary — goalsStore, sequencesStore, automationsStore Migrated

## One-liner
Migrated three secondary stores to Supabase: sales_goals, email_sequences/sequence_enrollments, and automation_rules tables.

## What was done
- **goalsStore**: removed persist, added `fetchGoals()`, optimistic CRUD, `rowToGoal` mapper. Table: `sales_goals`
- **sequencesStore**: removed persist, added `fetchSequences()` fetching both `email_sequences` and `sequence_enrollments` in parallel, full CRUD for sequences and enrollments
- **automationsStore**: removed persist + `onRehydrateStorage`, added `fetchRules()`, kept `executeRulesForTrigger` cross-store side effects intact. Table: `automation_rules`
- All three added to `useDataInit.ts` and `realtimeSubscriptions.ts`

## Result
Goals, sequences, and automations persist in Supabase with seed fallback.
